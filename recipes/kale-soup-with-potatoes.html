Kale Soup with Potatoes

A simple recipe with simple ingredients to delight in a simple way!

Ingredients (for one person)

  • 6 medium potatoes peeled and chopped
  • 1 bunch of washed and chopped kale
  • ½ finely chopped onion
  • ½ tablespoon of salt and chopped garlic
  • 1 chicken bouillon

You can chop the potatoes and kale roughly, leaving large pieces.

Directions

  1. Put some oil in a pan.

    2. add the onion, the garlic with salt and wait until the garlic is golden.

  2. Put the potatoes and fill with water.
  3. Add chicken bouillon and cover the pan.

    4. leave the heat on high.

  4. When potatoes soften, add the kale.

    5. and stir for approximately 1 minute until it wilts.

  5. Turn off the heat and cool the soup.
  6. After it cools, add to the blender and blend.
  7. Return the soup to the pot and add your preferred protein.
